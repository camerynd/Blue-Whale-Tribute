Some Facts About the Blue Whale

  • Blue whales can grow up to 105 feet long and weigh up to 199 tonnes
  • Blue whales are larger than any dinosaur
  • Blue whales can live up to 90 years and have only one offspring at a time
  • In infancy, the blue whale drinks milk from it's mother, and once developed enough, it will start eating solid foods as well as hunting for its own prey
  • Their diet consists mostly of krill, a small shrimp-like crustacean
